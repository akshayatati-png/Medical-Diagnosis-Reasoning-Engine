Diseases & Solutions

Dengue

Viral disease caused by mosquito bites. Common symptoms include fever, skin rash, headache, and joint pain.

Recommended Solution

IV Fluids, Hydration Therapy, and Paracetamol.

Malaria

Mosquito-borne infection affecting blood cells. Causes fever, chills, sweating, and muscle pain.

Recommended Solution

Artemisinin Combination Therapy and anti-malarial medications.

COVID-19

Respiratory infection affecting lungs. Symptoms include dry cough, fatigue, and breathlessness.

Recommended Solution

Oxygen Support, antiviral treatment, and physician care.

Typhoid

Bacterial infection spread through contaminated food. Causes abdominal pain, weakness, and prolonged fever.

Recommended Solution

Azithromycin, Ceftriaxone, and hydration.

Chikungunya

Mosquito-borne viral disease causing swollen joints, fever, and severe joint pain.

Recommended Solution

Rest therapy, hydration, and anti-inflammatory medicines.
